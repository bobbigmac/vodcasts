# Tweets Shows

Curated pack: 50 tweet drafts built to showcase the broader video library.
These lean toward church services, sermon series, Bible teaching, worship-friendly weekend channels, and calmer mainstream picks.
All links use modern `prays.be` routes and point at video-backed feeds from the current build.
