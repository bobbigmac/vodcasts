**Copy to use (press release body template)**
```text
Headline: Prays.Be makes it easier to watch and follow Christian sermons and teaching in one place

[City, Date] — Prays.Be (https://prays.be/) is a distraction-free way to watch and listen to Christian sermons and teachings online. Built around public sermon/vodcast feeds, it organizes teaching into a simple TV-style experience so people can browse, pick a message, and continue where they left off.

Unlike social video platforms, Prays.Be focuses on a clean guide-style interface designed for services, sermons, and teaching. It’s useful for anyone who wants a straightforward way to keep up with sermon series, revisit messages, or discover teaching across multiple ministries without relying on recommendations or endless feeds.

Prays.Be is available on the web at https://prays.be/

About Prays.Be
Prays.Be is a minimal “vodcast TV” style player for Christian sermons and teaching, organized from public feeds into a simple browsing and playback experience.
```
