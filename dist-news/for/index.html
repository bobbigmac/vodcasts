Who it’s for

Prays.be is for people who want a simple, respectful way to watch and listen to faith-based content — whether that’s sermons, Bible teaching, worship, testimony, or thoughtful talks.

Our tone

We’re aiming for wholesome, hopeful, and grounded. We don’t want doom-scrolling. We want something you can put on at home, on the train, or on a break — and feel better for it.

Limitations

Because content comes from many third-party feeds, availability and quality can vary. Some items may disappear, change, or fail to play. We’ll keep improving the experience.
