About Prays.be

Prays.be is a free, lightweight way to stream faith-based video and audio content from many different sources in one place.

Our goal is simple: make it easier to find something encouraging, thoughtful, and faith-forward — on any device.

What you can do here

  • Browse shows by category and channel.
  • Pick up where you left off (saved on your device).
  • Share links to feeds, shows, and episodes.

About sources

We link to third-party feeds. Some sources may be imperfect or controversial; we’re not here to point fingers — we’re here to build a calm, useful way to watch and listen. Over time, we may add clearer source notes and improve curation.

We may adjust which feeds are included at any time.

Removal requests

If you run a feed and would prefer not to be included, email admin@prays.be and we’ll remove it.
